The Flats at Norman

Campus living with all of the high-end accoutrements.

Location

Tucked away behind the Everest Training Center, just a stone throw away is The Flats at Norman apartment complex. The Flats stand out for the proximity to the heart of OU’s campus—it is right around .05 miles away! Einstein Bagels is on the way for all the coffee lovers that need a pick-me-up on the way to those dreaded morning classes.

Amenities

The Flats has all the bells and whistles—from a heavily populated basketball court to a beautiful pool complete with waterfalls and a giant, 60 inch TV and state-of-the-art grill for food aficionados and hungry people alike, The Flats has it all! The crowning jewel of this stunning complex is the newly renovated gym—it was remodeled in November 2024!

What's so special about The Flats?

The thing that makes The Flats so special is the price. For a two-bedroom, two-bath space, you will end up paying less than $1000! It comes partly furnished with a couch, fridge, desk and your very own personal mini fridge. The Flats also has an on-site maintenance service that is never more than a phone call away. If this sounds appealing to you, don’t wait! The rooms are usually filled up by mid-July!
