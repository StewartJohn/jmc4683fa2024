Jimmie Austin OU Golf Club

Premiere 18-hole PGA course

What is it?

Jimmie Austin Golf Club , located in Norman, Oklahoma, is a premier 18-hole golf course known for its scenic beauty, challenging design, and exceptional facilities. Originally opened in 1954, the course has undergone several renovations over the years, enhancing both its playability and aesthetic appeal. The course is set against the backdrop of the picturesque rolling hills and wooded terrain of the Oklahoma countryside, providing a tranquil and visually stunning environment for golfers of all skill levels. With its well-maintained fairways and greens, Jimmie Austin is regarded as one of the top golf courses in the region.

Features

The course itself is a championship-level layout, measuring 7,450 yards from the championship tees. Designed by architect Robert Cupp, it offers a strategic blend of open fairways, strategically placed hazards, and elevation changes that challenge both beginners and seasoned golfers alike. In addition to its impressive design, the golf club boasts a state-of-the-art driving range, putting greens, and short game areas, allowing golfers to hone their skills before hitting the course. The course also hosts the annual University of Oklahoma men’s and women’s golf teams, offering a top-tier venue for collegiate golf competitions.

Amenities

Beyond the golf course, Jimmie Austin Golf Club provides a range of amenities that elevate the overall experience. The club features a clubhouse with a pro shop, a full-service restaurant, and event spaces for weddings and other special occasions. Golfers can enjoy a meal or a drink while taking in views of the course, making it an ideal place for socializing after a round. Whether you're an avid golfer looking for a challenging game or simply seeking a beautiful location to relax and enjoy the outdoors, Jimmie Austin Golf Club in Norman is a top-tier destination for both recreation and relaxation.
