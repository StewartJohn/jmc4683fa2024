Logie's on the Corner

Traditional college town vibes and drinks

What is it?

Logie's on the Corner, located in the heart of Norman, Oklahoma, is a popular bar known for its lively atmosphere, extensive drink menu, and unbeatable location near the University of Oklahoma campus. This hotspot combines the energy of a sports bar with the welcoming charm of a neighborhood pub. Logie’s features a spacious interior, a large outdoor patio, and multiple TVs, making it an ideal spot for catching a game, socializing with friends, or winding down after a busy day. Its signature drinks, daily specials, and hearty bar food menu keep patrons coming back for more.

About

Logie's on the Corner is part of a regional chain of bars with a strong following, particularly in college towns. The Norman location, opened in 2005, quickly cemented its reputation as a favorite among students, alumni, and locals alike. Known for fostering a fun, casual environment, Logie’s has become synonymous with good times and great drinks. Its connection to the community, especially during game days and special events, has made it a staple in Norman’s bustling nightlife scene.

Events

Logie's thrives on creating memorable experiences for its patrons, hosting events like trivia nights, live music, themed parties, and watch parties for OU sports. The bar’s vibrant energy, friendly staff, and affordable pricing earn it consistent praise in online reviews. Guests often highlight the welcoming vibe, the quality of the drink specials, and the great view of the game from anywhere in the bar. Whether it’s a pre-game meetup, a night out with friends, or just a place to relax with a cold drink, Logie’s on the Corner delivers a fun and energetic atmosphere that embodies the spirit of Norman.
