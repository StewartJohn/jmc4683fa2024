Love's Field

A new and updated home for OU Women's Softball

About

Love's Field is located off of Jenkins, next to the Lloyd Noble Center and L. Dale Mitchell Baseball Park. Love’s Field holds 4,200 people and is 44,000 square feet. The field features a huge, Oklahoma shaped, score board, as well as plenty of space to walk, talk and watch the game.

History

Love’s Field is the home of the eight time NCAA Women’s College World Series champions, Oklahoma Sooners softball. Love’s Field is the newest of any stadium or center here at OU, and is sponsored by Love’s Travel Spots. The stadium opened March 1, 2024 en route to the Sooner’s eighth national championship.

Events

Love’s Field is mainly for softball games, but there is a possibility we could see other events be hosted here. There are seats as well as standing room to watch games and interact with other fans. There are concessions for food and beverages for all ages.
