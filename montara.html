Montara Park

Quiet living at a competitive price

Location

Montara Park (formerly known as Campus Lodge), is located around two miles away from the main campus. It’s quiet and out of the way from that bustling thoroughfare on East Lindsey Street! It is a bit of a walk to campus, but if you have a car, bike or scooter, it might just be the place for you! There is a Dunkin Donuts right next to the complex just in case you need some morning class fuel!

Amenities

The first thing I noticed about this place was the living room — it is very spacious! Each apartment has a balcony so you can view those gorgeous Oklahoma sunrises from the comfort of your own home. The lodge is also a nice touch, with a pool table, free coffee, a nice-sized swimming pool and a newly renovated gym for all of your fitness needs!

What's special about Montara Park?

The price is very affordable, with the current going rate for a four-bedroom four-bathroom suite being around $505-$595. The complex provides roommates based on a selective processing form if you’re not able to bring your friends. Check out some glistening reviews for this hidden gem!
