Pickleman's Gourmet Cafe

Great pizza and sandwiches at all hours of the day

What is it?

Pickleman's Gourmet Cafe in Norman, Oklahoma, is a popular spot for fresh, high-quality sandwiches, salads, and soups served in a casual, inviting setting. Located near the University of Oklahoma campus, it has become a favorite for students, locals, and visitors seeking quick and delicious meals. Known for its toasted sandwiches, artisan ingredients, and flavorful dressings, Pickleman’s offers a menu that caters to a variety of tastes and dietary preferences. Whether you’re dining in, taking out, or ordering delivery, the cafe’s fast and friendly service ensures a satisfying experience.

About

Pickleman’s Gourmet Cafe was founded in 2005 in Columbia, Missouri, with a vision to provide fresh and affordable gourmet dining options. The Norman location, part of this growing franchise, has established itself as a staple in the community since its opening in 2009. With its commitment to using quality ingredients, including bread baked fresh daily and premium meats and cheeses, Pickleman’s has carved out a niche in Norman’s competitive dining scene. The restaurant has consistently upheld its brand's mission to offer a gourmet experience at an accessible price point.

Events

Pickleman’s in Norman regularly engages with the local community by offering catering services for events and supporting campus and community initiatives. Its convenient location and reliable quality make it a go-to for quick lunches, late-night cravings, and group gatherings. Customers frequently praise its efficient service, fresh ingredients, and the standout flavors of menu items like the Toasted Italian sandwich and Chipotle Chicken pizza. Online reviews often highlight the cafe’s welcoming atmosphere and the consistency that keeps patrons returning time and again. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a loyal fan, Pickleman’s delivers a gourmet twist on classic comfort food.
