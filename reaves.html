Reaves Park

Family friendly destination with playground and fairs

What is it?

Reaves Park, located in the heart of Norman, Oklahoma, is a popular community park known for its wide array of recreational facilities and scenic green spaces. Spanning over 45 acres, the park is a hub for outdoor activities and offers something for everyone. Visitors can enjoy expansive open fields, playgrounds, picnic areas, and walking trails. Its central location makes it an ideal spot for both locals and visitors to relax, exercise, or gather for social events. The park’s natural beauty is complemented by well-maintained landscaping, providing a peaceful setting for all who come to enjoy it.

Features

One of the park's standout features is its extensive sports amenities. Reaves Park boasts multiple athletic fields, including soccer fields, softball diamonds, and tennis courts. These facilities are frequently used for organized sports leagues, casual games, and individual practice. A large basketball court and a sand volleyball court further enhance the park’s appeal to sports enthusiasts. Whether you're looking to join a pickup game or cheer on a local team, Reaves Park provides an accessible and well-equipped environment for athletic activities.

Events

In addition to its outdoor offerings, Reaves Park hosts seasonal events and community gatherings, making it a focal point of local culture. The park features a large, covered pavilion that is often used for family reunions, birthday parties, and other celebrations. With its welcoming atmosphere, diverse facilities, and central location, Reaves Park has become a beloved gathering place in Norman, offering a blend of leisure, fitness, and community engagement. Whether enjoying a leisurely afternoon or participating in a competitive game, the park is an important part of the city’s recreational landscape.
