Tarahumara's Mexican Cafe & Cantina

Traditional Mexican cuisine for all

What is it?

Tarahumara's Mexican Cafe & Cantina is a beloved dining destination known for its authentic Mexican cuisine and vibrant atmosphere. Located in the heart of Norman, Oklahoma, the restaurant offers a menu filled with traditional Mexican dishes, including enchiladas, tacos, and tamales, along with signature salsas and freshly made tortillas. Its cantina features a wide array of tequila-based cocktails, margaritas, and Mexican beers, making it a lively spot for both casual dining and celebrations. The warm, colorful decor and friendly service ensure that every visit feels like a fiesta.

About

The restaurant takes its name and inspiration from the Tarahumara people, an indigenous group native to Mexico's Copper Canyon region. Since its establishment in 2001, Tarahumara's has aimed to honor this heritage by showcasing the flavors and cooking techniques rooted in Mexican tradition. Over the years, it has become a staple in the community, attracting both locals and visitors with its commitment to quality and authenticity. The owners pride themselves on using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and incorporating family recipes passed down through generations.
