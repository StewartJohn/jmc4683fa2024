Torchy's Tacos

A comfortable hangout for brews and bites

What is it?

Torchy’s Tacos in Norman, Oklahoma, is a vibrant and trendy spot known for its bold, creative approach to tacos and a lively bar atmosphere. Located near the University of Oklahoma, it’s a favorite for students, locals, and visitors looking for unique flavor combinations and a relaxed setting. The menu features a wide array of tacos, from the classic and comforting to the adventurous and spicy, along with mouthwatering queso, fresh guacamole, and indulgent desserts. The full-service bar offers a curated selection of craft cocktails, margaritas, and local brews, making it an ideal spot for casual dining, happy hours, and late-night gatherings.

About

Torchy's Tacos began as a humble food truck in Austin, Texas, in 2006, with a mission to create “damn good tacos” using fresh, high-quality ingredients. Over the years, it expanded across multiple states, bringing its bold flavors and cheeky personality to communities like Norman. Since opening in 2016, the Norman location has quickly become a hotspot, blending the brand’s signature creativity with a welcoming vibe that resonates with locals. The combination of innovative recipes and a fun, laid-back atmosphere has solidified Torchy’s as more than just a taco joint, it’s a destination.

Events

Torchy’s Tacos is known for its dynamic events and promotions, including Taco of the Month specials, happy hour deals, and festive celebrations on Cinco de Mayo and other holidays. The Norman location often buzzes with activity, attracting crowds for casual lunches, pre-game gatherings, and evening drinks. Reviews frequently highlight the crave-worthy tacos such as the Trailer Park and Brushfire and the irresistible Green Chile Queso as standout favorites. Diners also praise the friendly staff and lively ambiance, which combine to create a memorable experience. Torchy’s Tacos continues to be a go-to spot in Norman for bold flavors and good times.
