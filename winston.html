The Winston

Dining and drinks with a heavy helping of class

What is it?

The Winston , located in the heart of Norman, Oklahoma, is a stylish gastropub that combines upscale dining with a relaxed and inviting atmosphere. Known for its eclectic menu, The Winston offers everything from gourmet burgers and fresh salads to perfectly seared steaks and creative shareable plates. The bar is equally impressive, boasting a wide selection of craft cocktails, fine wines, and locally brewed beers. Its modern-industrial decor, complete with cozy lighting and comfortable seating, makes it an ideal spot for both casual outings and special occasions.

About

Named in honor of Winston Churchill, the restaurant reflects a commitment to excellence and sophistication while maintaining a friendly, approachable vibe. Since opening its doors in 2018, The Winston has become a staple of Norman's dining scene. Operated by the same group behind some of the city's most beloved eateries, it quickly earned a reputation for quality and innovation. Its commitment to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and continually evolving the menu ensures that guests always have something new and exciting to look forward to.

Events

The Winston is not only a go to dining destination but also a popular venue for events and gatherings. From weekly happy hour specials and trivia nights to private events and seasonal celebrations, it has become a hub for community connection and entertainment. The restaurant regularly earns glowing reviews for its exceptional food, attentive service, and inviting atmosphere.
